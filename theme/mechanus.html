Mechanus and Modrons

Mechanus is the plane of pure law. Souls of people who sought rigid society become Modrons at the center of the plane. The Modrons are led by a mysterious entity known as Primus. Each Modron has a place, with the lowest answering to the one directly above it, and that one to the one above it, etc. Their purpose is to maintain the large clockwork cogs that make up the plane. Why this is needed, only Primus knows. Any influence from outside Mechanus brings chaos and is met with fear, revulsion, and in extreme cases, violence.
