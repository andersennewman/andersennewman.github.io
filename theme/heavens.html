The Heavens

The Heavens are a realm of pure good. Law and chaos soak the edges, creating realms of law and good and chaos and good.

Celestia and Angels

The realm of Celestia is a great mountain with seven peaks. It represents lawful good and the struggle to always push oneself further. It is guarded and looked over by angels, paragons of law and goodness.

Elysium

Elysium is the realm of purest good. It is a land of pure bliss and happiness.

Arvandor

Arvandor is the realm of freedom and benevolence. It is a place of warm forests and kind wanderers.

Olympus

Olympus is the second level of the Underspire. It is a warm, sunlit forest with few dangers. It is inhabited by fey and celestials, often helping wanderers find way. The dangers that do exist come from the evil fey that hide in the darkest parts of the woods. Fairgrove is the main town on this level, a place where folks help one another and respite waits for those who need it.
