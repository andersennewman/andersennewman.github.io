Limbo and Slaadi

Limbo is the plane of pure chaos. Slaadi inhabit the plane, gliding and jumping between the ever-changing chunks of matter that dot the plane. The Spawning Stone sits at the center of the plane, a relic of an ancient incursion by Mechanus. What was meant to create a modicum of rigidity to the plane instead began spawning the Slaad, embodiments of chaos, from the souls of the dead who strove for freedom above all else. The innate nature of the plane makes it nearly impossible for traversal by most.
